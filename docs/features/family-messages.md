# Family Messages

:::info Coming Soon
Family Messages is planned for a future release. We're designing a family messaging system based on feedback from early users.

**What we're planning:**
- Leave notes and reminders for family members
- Schedule messages for specific times
- Send to specific displays or all family members
- Read receipts when messages are seen

**Current alternatives:**
- Use shared Google Calendar events for reminders
- Add text content to displays manually
- Use Cozi lists for to-do items

**Want this feature sooner?**
Contact us at hello@familycast.app to express interest and share your use case. Your feedback helps us prioritize features.
:::

## Planned Features

When we launch Family Messages, it will include:

### Message Types
- Quick notes ("Snack in fridge")
- Daily reminders ("Don't forget your lunch!")
- Schedule changes ("Practice canceled today")
- Motivational messages ("You got this!")

### Targeting
- Send to specific child's display
- Send to all displays
- Send to specific rooms

### Scheduling
- Immediate messages
- Scheduled for specific times
- Recurring reminders

### Safety
- Parent-approved only (kids can't send without approval)
- Age-appropriate content filtering
- Message history for accountability
